# Using Research and Google Workspace

Updated over 5 months ago

In this video guide, we'll walk through using Claude's Research capabilities with the Google Workspace integration, allowing you to leverage your emails, calendar, documents and the web for comprehensive insights.

- Research is available for users with paid Claude plans (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise) using Claude on the web, Claude Desktop, or Claude Mobile.

- Google integrations are available for users on all paid Claude plans.

- You must have [web search turned on](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/10684626-enabling-and-using-web-search) for Research to function.
