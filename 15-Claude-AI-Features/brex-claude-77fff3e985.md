Connect Brex to Claude for automating your company's finances. Finance monitors compliance and runs powerful queries. Employees get personalized memo guidance, policy answers, and status updates. Permission-based access ensures admin oversight while streamlining management.

You can use Brex to:

**Check Card Information:**\
"Get details about my corporate card spending limits"

**Review Expense Policies:**\
"What are the current expense policies for travel and meals?"

**Track Reimbursements:**\
"When will my expense reimbursement be processed?"

**Monitor User Access:**\
"Show me all active users and their permissions in our Brex account"
