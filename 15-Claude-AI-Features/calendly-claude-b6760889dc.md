Schedule smarter with Calendly in Claude. Manage your entire scheduling workflow through simple conversation — create and update event types, share scheduling links, adjust availability, book meetings, and more, without ever leaving Claude.

You can use Calendly to:

**Create a scheduled event:**\
"Book a 30 min coaching session with User A ([\[email protected\]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)) for this Thursday at 2 PM."

**Find available time slots:**\
"Find open slots next week for my 30 min coaching session."

**Update availability:**\
"Remove Fridays as available days for scheduling 30 min coaching sessions."

**Generate scheduling links:**\
"Generate a one-time scheduling link for my 30 min coaching session."
