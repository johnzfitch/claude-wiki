Connect Claude to read, write, and act on 350+ enterprise data sources like Salesforce, Snowflake and NetSuite in real-time through one fully hosted remote MCP server. Connect AI is the only managed MCP platform built with semantic intelligence that understands your data's metadata and relationships across systems for multi-source analysis. Ensure trusted boundaries with enterprise security, granular permissions, and complete audit trails. Set up any source in minutes and get started free.

\
You can use the CData Connect AI connector to:

Analyze Campaign Performance:**\
‍**"Show me which marketing campaigns had the highest conversion rates last quarter and identify common patterns among top performers"

Get Audience Insights:**\
‍**"What are the demographic and behavioral trends of users who engaged with our recent email campaign?"

Track Pipeline Changes:**\
‍**"What deals moved stages this week and which ones are at risk of slipping?"

Identify Sales Trends:\
**‍**"Show me close-lost trends from the past quarter and suggest coaching opportunities for underperforming reps"

Generate Financial Reports:**\
‍**"Create a summary report of Q3 expenses by department with year-over-year comparisons"

Get Real-Time Budget Updates:**\
‍**"How much of our marketing budget have we spent this month and what's remaining for each campaign?"

Run Financial Scenarios:**\
‍**"Run a what-if scenario showing how a 15% increase in operational costs would impact our quarterly projections"
