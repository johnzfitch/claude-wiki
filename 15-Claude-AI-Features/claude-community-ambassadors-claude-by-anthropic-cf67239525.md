## What you get

The Claude Community Ambassadors program gives you everything you need to create a thriving community of builders in your city.

### Grow your local community

Host meetups, workshops, and hackathons that bring builders in your city together. We cover event funding, ready-to-use content, swag, and promotion through Anthropic's channels.

### Build and experiment

Stay sharp and teach from experience. Run live demos, prototype ideas, and share what you know with your community. Get monthly API credits from Anthropic to power your demos.

### Shape the product

Bring your community's perspective to Anthropic’s product teams. Access pre-release features and Builders Council sessions to influence what gets built.

### Connect and contribute

Share what's working in your city and help shape how this program grows through a private Slack with fellow Ambassadors and the Anthropic team.

## Our ambassadors

If you’re active in your community and want to go further with Anthropic's support, we encourage you to apply. No developer title required.

### Community builders

You bring people together. You organize meetups, lead local groups, or hold a visible presence on Discord, Reddit, or X.

### Technical users

You have hands-on experience with Claude Code or Claude Cowork and can help others get started and go deeper.

### Claude advocates

You're genuinely excited about Claude and Anthropic's mission. You help others learn naturally and give honest feedback.

## FAQ

### Is there a cost to join?

No. Event sponsorship, API credits, swag, and resources and more are all provided at no cost to you.

### Do I need to be a professional developer? 

No. You should have meaningful experience with Claude Code or Claude Cowork and a track record of community involvement, but a developer title isn't required.

### Do I need to live in a specific location? 

No. The program is global and we actively prioritize geographic diversity. Multiple Ambassadors can operate in the same city.

### Will I receive compensation?

The program doesn't include direct payment. Benefits include event sponsorship, API credits, networking, swag, and recognition.

### How long does membership last? 

Membership is ongoing as long as you're hosting events and staying engaged. If circumstances change, you can step away and reapply when you're ready.

### Can I be an ambassador for other companies? 

Yes, in most cases. We ask that you're not also an ambassador for a company whose core business competes with Anthropic's. If unsure, mention it in your application and we'll follow up.

### Can I talk about being an Ambassador publicly? 

Yes! We encourage you to share it on social media, your resume, and LinkedIn.
