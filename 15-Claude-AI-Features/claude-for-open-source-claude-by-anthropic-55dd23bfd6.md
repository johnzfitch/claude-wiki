# Thank you for everything you ship. Claude Max is on us.

Open-source maintainers and contributors keep the ecosystem running. The Claude for Open Source Program is our way of saying thank you for all your hard work, with 6 months of free Claude Max 20x. Apply now.

## Who should apply:

- ‍**Maintainers:** You’re a primary maintainer or core team member of a public repo with 5,000+ GitHub stars *or* 1M+ monthly NPM downloads. You've made**‍** commits, releases, or PR reviews within the last 3 months.
- ‍**Don't quite fit the criteria** If you maintain something the ecosystem quietly depends on, apply anyway and tell us about it.
