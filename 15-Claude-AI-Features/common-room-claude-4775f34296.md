Query your Common Room using natural language. Search contacts by title, segment, or company. List organizations by size or industry. Browse activities by keyword or time range. Filter, sort, and get the details you need—names, titles, emails, company info—without leaving the conversation.

You can use Common Room to:

**Find High-Intent Contacts:**\
"Find contacts at enterprise companies who visited our pricing page this week"

**Filter by Segment and Role:**\
"Show me all engineering managers in my 'Product Champions' segment"

**Account-Based Queries:**\
"Who from Acme Corp has been active in our Slack community?"

**Surface At-Risk Accounts:**\
"List contacts from accounts in my 'At Risk' segment"
