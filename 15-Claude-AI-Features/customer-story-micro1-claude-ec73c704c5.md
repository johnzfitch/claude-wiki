micro1 uses Claude to conduct thousands of AI-powered technical interviews daily, helping companies evaluate global talent while providing candidates with an unbiased, lower pressure interview experience.

With Claude, micro1:

- Conducts over 3,000 AI interviews per day
- Reduces recruitment costs by 85% compared to traditional methods
- Achieves 5x higher human interview pass rates
- Enables teams like Legal Soft to maintain 10,000 monthly interviews with 64% less staff time

## The challenge of transforming technical recruitment

Traditional recruiting methods have reached their breaking point. Companies feel pressure to hire exceptional talent while drowning in inefficient processes. Resumes alone cannot determine interview eligibility, while manual screening calls consume countless recruiter hours. Take-home tests fare no better—with up to 85% of candidates using AI to complete exercises, these assessments provide little real insight into abilities.

The cost is also staggering. Companies like Legal Soft previously needed 33 staff members just to handle screening interviews. This time-intensive process often fails to identify the best candidates, with qualified applicants overlooked because recruiters lack the bandwidth to properly evaluate everyone.

micro1 recognized that solving this challenge required more than incremental improvements. They aim to make every recruiter on the planet 100x more powerful through AI-powered interviewing. This means reimagining the entire technical assessment process to create a scalable system that maintains the high standards companies require for technical hires.

## Choosing Claude for intelligent interviewing

After evaluating multiple AI models, micro1 selected Claude, citing three key factors. First, Claude demonstrated superior understanding of technical concepts. "Claude stands out for its general intelligence and ability to truly understand technical queries," said Aruj Mahajan, Lead Engineer at micro1.

Second, the implementation process was straightforward. Mahajan said, "The documentation is very well presented, and the prompting guidelines helped us get exactly what we wanted."

Finally, Claude's ethical foundation aligned with micro1’s values. Sydney Levin, Head of Partnerships at micro1, said, "We really value Anthropic's commitment to ethical standards as a safety-minded research company."

## How Claude powers AI-driven interviews

micro1's platform uses Claude to:

- Evaluate technical skills through dynamic conversations
- Provide consistent, unbiased assessments
- Create anxiety-free interview experiences
- Generate comprehensive interview reports
- Enable 24/7 candidate evaluation across time zones

## Transforming companies through efficient, unbiased technical hiring

micro1's impact spans companies of all sizes. At Deel, where 85% of candidates used AI for take-home tests, micro1's AI interviewer increased human interview pass rates from 10% to 50%. This improvement helped Deel reduce recruitment costs by over 80% while improving candidate quality.

Legal Soft demonstrates the platform's scalability. By implementing micro1, they streamlined their interviewing team from 33 to 12 while maintaining 10,000 monthly candidate screenings. This efficiency improved their EBITDA margins by 30% and saved over \$400,000 annually in recruitment costs.

For micro1, success goes beyond metrics. William Almeida, CRO at micro1, said, "Our goal is to empower recruiters. We want to 10x their efficiency." The platform transforms recruiters into strategic operators who can focus on high-value decisions rather than routine screenings.

Most importantly, the system creates fairer opportunities for candidates. Almeida said, "We want to give everyone a voice. We know that there are tons of talent that can't sell themselves properly, so we give them the opportunity to define the areas they excel at." Through unbiased AI evaluation, micro1 ensures every candidate gets a fair chance to demonstrate their abilities.

## Building the future of technical recruiting

micro1 envisions a future where every qualified candidate gets a fair chance, regardless of their background or ability to craft the perfect resume. They continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in technical recruiting through their partnership with Anthropic.

micro1 aims to transform lives by democratizing access to opportunities. By combining Claude's AI capabilities with their commitment to fair evaluation, micro1 works to create a future where exceptional talent never goes undiscovered. Together with Anthropic, they're not just changing how companies hire—they're changing who gets the chance to be hired in the first place.
