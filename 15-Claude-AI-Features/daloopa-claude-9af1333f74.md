Supplies high quality fundamental data sourced from SEC Filings, investor presentations and any public financial documents, with hyperlinks to source document overlaying each data point. Provides a zero-hallucination foundation for any quantitative financial analysis in Claude.\
\
You can use the Daloopa connector to:\
\
Build comparative dashboards:\
"Build a comparative dashboard for MSFT, ORCL, SAP, comparing cloud ARR growth, gross dollar retention and EBIT margin for the last 3 years. Break this out by quarters and show me both the data and growth rates calculated from the data. Use Daloopa data and provide hyperlinks"

Generate due diligence reports:\
"Generate a research report that summarizes all key historical trends for NVDIA, and specifically highlight changes in margins and pricing. Please break out which segments drove major changes for both. Use Daloopa data and provide hyperlinks"

Track inflection points:\
"For VRT, highlight key Q/Q changes from the most recent financial data compared to the previous quarter. Walk me through the main accels and decels and margin changes. Identify macroeconomic and company specific drivers of these trends. Use Daloopa data and provide hyperlinks"

Perform complex financial analysis:\
"Run me a sensitivity analysis on ARPU and impacts on fiscal year 26 profit margins for NFLX. Use only Non-GAAP data. Use Daloopa data and provide hyperlinks"
