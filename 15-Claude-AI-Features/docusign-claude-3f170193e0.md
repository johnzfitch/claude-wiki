Simplify contract management with intelligent automation powered by Docusign. Go beyond simple execution to make complex agreement workflows effortless. Use natural language to connect with your agreement data, instantly surface insights like renewal dates and key obligations, and create automated workflows that keep your business moving faster and with less friction.

You can use Docusign to:

**Create & Send Agreements:**\
"Create a contract from our Master Service Agreement template, fill in the business details, and send it to Legal for review."

**Review Agreements:**\
"Review this service contract, suggest edits to align with company policy, and start a vendor review."

**Search & Surface Agreements:**\
"Show me customer contracts expiring in the next 90 days with a price increase clause."

**Workflow Management:**\
"Trigger the customer onboarding workflow and notify me when identity verification is complete."
