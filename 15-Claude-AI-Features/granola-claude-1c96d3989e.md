Connect Claude to your meeting history. Ask questions about your work and use your conversation context to get things done.

You can use Granola to:

**Search across meetings:**\
"Find all meetings where we discussed the Q1 product roadmap"

**Review meeting details:**\
"Show me the transcript from my last standup with the engineering team"

**Get contextual answers:**\
"What action items were assigned to me across this week's meetings?"

**List recent meetings:**\
"List all my meetings from the past week and summarize the key decisions"
