# Intro to Artifacts

Updated this week

In this video guide, we'll explore how Artifacts can enrich your interactions with Claude, allowing for more dynamic and interactive conversations. Before you get started, review the availability of the features demonstrated in this video:

- Artifacts are available on all claude.ai plans. More information on enabling/disabling this feature can be found [here](https://support.anthropic.com/en/articles/9487308-how-do-i-enable-or-disable-the-artifacts-feature).

- Projects are available on Claude Pro and Claude for Work (Team & Enterprise plans).
