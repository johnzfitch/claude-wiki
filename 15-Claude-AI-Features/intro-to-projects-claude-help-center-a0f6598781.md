# Intro to Projects

Updated this week

In this video guide, we'll explore how Projects can streamline your workflow with Claude, allowing for more organized and efficient conversations. Before you get started, review the availability of the features demonstrated in this video:

- Projects are available to all users, including those with free Claude accounts. Free users can create a maximum of five projects.

- Project sharing features are available on Team and Enterprise plans. More information on visibility and sharing can be found [here](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9519189-project-visibility-and-sharing).
