Brings flight search directly into Claude. The Kiwi connector supports one-way/round-trip, flexible dates, multiple passengers, all cabin classes. Works with Claude and Claude Desktop.\
\
You can use the Kiwi connector to:

Find Last-Minute Weekend Getaways:**\
‍**"Find cheap flights this weekend from Vienna to somewhere warm, under €150"

Plan Family Trips with Specific Needs:**\
‍**"Family of four, Barcelona to Rome, December 15-22, need morning departures due to traveling with young children"

Explore Budget Travel Destinations:**\
‍**"Where can I fly from Amsterdam in March for under €100 round trip?"

Search Flexible Date Options:**\
‍**"Show me the cheapest flights from London to Tokyo anytime in April"
