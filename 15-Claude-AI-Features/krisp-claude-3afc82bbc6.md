Connect your meeting history to AI tools so past decisions, action items, and conversation context are easy to find and use.

You can use Krisp to:

**Search Meeting History:**\
"Find all meetings where we discussed the Q4 budget"

**Track Action Items:**\
"Show me all pending action items from last week's meetings"

**Get Meeting Context:**\
"What decisions were made in yesterday's product planning meeting?"

**Upcoming Meeting Prep:**\
"What should I prepare for my 3pm meeting with the marketing team?"
