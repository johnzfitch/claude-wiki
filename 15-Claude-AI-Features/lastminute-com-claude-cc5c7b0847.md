Access real-time flight data directly through Claude to search, compare, and book flights across global airlines. Get instant price comparisons, flexible filters, and intelligent recommendations that bring Claude's capabilities into your travel planning. Seamlessly enhance your workflow with comprehensive flight search powered by lastminute.com.

You can use lastminute.com to:

**Flight Search:**"Find flights from London to Barcelona next weekend"

**Price Comparison:**"Compare round-trip flights from NYC to Paris in June"

**Flexible Planning:**"Show me the cheapest flights to anywhere in Europe from Berlin in March"

**Multi-city Search:**"Find flights for a trip from San Francisco to Tokyo to Sydney"
