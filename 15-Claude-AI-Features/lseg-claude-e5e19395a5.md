Provides real-time access to LSEG's comprehensive financial market data ecosystem, spanning across asset classes and domains. It enables seamless integration of institutional-grade market data, analytics, and valuation tools directly into conversational workflows, allowing users to access deep market insights, perform complex calculations, and analyze financial instruments through natural language interactions.

You can use the LSEG connector to:\
\
Get current FX spot rates:\
"What is the current EUR/GBP spot rate?"

Check historical spot rates:\
"What was the GBP spot rate on July 2nd, 2025?"

Price FX forward rates:\
"What's the value of the aussie tomnext?"

Calculate forward tenors:\
"What's the price of a 1 Month EURCHF forward?"

Price forwards via cross rates:\
"What's the price of a 30D NOKSEK forward via EUR?"

Get historical forward prices:\
"How did a 1M EUR / CHF forward look like on 14/11/2025?"

Plot interest rate curves:\
"I'd like to plot the USD SOFR swap curve"

List available interest rate curves:\
"What are the interest rate curves covered by LSEG?"

Get FX forward curves:\
"Can I get the AUD / USD fx curve using AONIA / SOFR cbs?"

Calculate bond yields:\
"What's the yield of FR0014012II5?"

Get bond descriptions and overviews:\
"Can you provide me with an overview of FR0014012II5?"

Display bond cash flows:\
"Can you display the cash flows of FR10YT=RR?"

Calculate spread measures:\
"Can I get the spread measure of FR0014012II5 for the current market price?"

Price bonds for specific spreads:\
"What would be the price of FR0014012II5 for a z-spread of 90?"

Calculate yields using custom conventions:\
"Can you provide the yield of US91282CNT44 using a Japanese simple convention?"
