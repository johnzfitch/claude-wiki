Connect your Mem notes and knowledge base to AI. Save ideas, plans, and context directly from your conversations, keep them organized into collections, then pick them up anytime.

You can use Mem to:

**Search and expand your knowledge:**\
"Search for Q2 pricing strategy notes, do deep research to validate our thinking, and summarize your findings."

**Save conversations as notes:**\
"Create a note from this chat with action items at the top."

**Maintain living documents:**\
"Find my note titled Q2 Pricing Plan, append our decisions from this chat, and add the research rationale at the bottom."

**Organize your workspace:**\
"Create a collection called Q2 Pricing Strategy and add the note you just updated, along with any other relevant notes."

**Manage tasks and priorities:**\
"List notes updated this week that contain open tasks, prioritize them based on urgency, and add them to a new note called Team Priorities - Week of Feb 23."
