Check your balance, download statements, search, analyze, and graph transactions, and understand Cards directly from Claude. Mercury brings your finances into your AI tools.

You can use Mercury to:

**Balance Check:**"What's the current balance across all my Mercury accounts?"

**Transaction Analysis:**"Show me all transactions over \$5,000 from last month"

**Statement Download:**"Download my account statement for Q4 2024"

**Card Management:**"List all active cards on my account and their spending limits"
