Connect AI assistants to your MotherDuck data warehouse. Explore and analyze data, create elegant visualizations, and share with colleagues using natural language—no SQL skills required. Works with messy, real-world data without requiring a semantic model or pre-configuration. The server provides safe, read-only access to your databases: your AI assistant acts like a data analyst, iterating through exploration, validation, and analysis to surface useful answers for any type of user.

You can use MotherDuck to:

**Data Exploration:**"List all tables in my sales database and describe their columns"

**Natural Language Queries:**"Show me the top 10 customers by revenue last quarter"

**Documentation Help:**"How do I create a time-series analysis in MotherDuck?"

**Catalog Search:**"Search my data catalog for anything related to customer churn"
