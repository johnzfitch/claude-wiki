Access an expanding range of MSCI AI-enabled data and analytics capabilities. Clients can use natural language queries to explore our equity index data and support materials based on their licensed content. Users can investigate and compare MSCI index performance characteristics such as country, sector and factor exposures, constituent weights and methodologies. Designed for investment professionals who need transparent and high-quality index data and insights for portfolio construction, attribution and exposure analysis.

You can use MSCI for:

**Portfolio Analysis:**\
"Show me the sector exposure breakdown for the MSCI World Index"

**Index Comparison:**\
"Compare the performance characteristics of MSCI Emerging Markets vs MSCI World over the last 5 years"

**Factor Analysis:**\
"What are the top 10 constituents in the MSCI USA Quality Index and their weights?"

**Methodology Research:**\
"Explain the methodology behind MSCI ESG ratings and how they impact index construction"
