You can use the NetSuite connector to:\
\
Query sales data:\
"How much did American Meats Inc buy from us in August?"

Run comprehensive account analysis:\
"Provide a detailed full account analysis"

Filter and retrieve sales orders:\
"Show me all sales orders from Q3 with a value over \$50,000"
