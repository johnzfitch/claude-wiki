Owkin builds AI agents for biology to accelerate drug discovery and de-risk clinical trials. Use Owkin to transform H&E slides from the TCGA database into granular, queryable insights. Researchers can quantify distinct cell types, analyze complex spatial tumor microenvironments, and validate hypotheses through cohort-level survival analysis—bridging the gap between raw biological data and actionable patient stratification.

You can use Owkin to:

**Refine Patient Stratification:\
‍**"I'm looking for Lung Adenocarcinoma patients that might be resistant to immunotherapy. Are there cases with low immune infiltration in the TCGA cohort?"

**Visualize Whole-Slide Images:\
‍**"Find the slide most enriched in eosinophils from cohort TCGA_BRCA and plot it."

**Assess Prognostic Value of H&E Based Markers:\
‍**"Is the density of plasmocytes associated with overall survival in bladder carcinoma?"

**Extract Quantitative Evidence for Reproducibility:\
‍**"Export the breakdown of all cell types for patient TCGA-A2-A0YI-01Z-00-DX1.1CF2EC2D-C722-467F-8832-409B823E8D8F.svs in parquet format, so I can reproduce this analysis."

**Understand Pathology Explorer Capabilities:\
‍**"Can you provide an overview of the Pathology Explorer tool and its capabilities?"
