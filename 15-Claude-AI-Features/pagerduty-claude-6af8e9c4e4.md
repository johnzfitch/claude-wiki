Manage incidents, create services, schedule overrides, and track on-call rotations with your PagerDuty account

You can use PagerDuty to:

**Incident Management:**\
"Create a new incident for the database outage and assign it to the infrastructure team"

**On-call Scheduling:**\
"Create a schedule override for this weekend and put Sarah on-call instead of John"

**Team Management:**\
"Add the new DevOps engineer to the infrastructure response team"

**Status Updates:**\
"Add a note to the current incident explaining the root cause and resolution steps"
