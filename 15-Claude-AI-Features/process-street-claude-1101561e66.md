Ask questions about your workflows, runs, tasks, users, and data sets, and take action on them safely. You can retrieve workflow details, inspect run history, check task status, update form fields, trigger new runs, and complete tasks. This provides real operational context and a controllable, auditable way to work inside your existing processes, making it easier for teams to get answers and take action directly within Process Street.

You can use Process Street to:

**Run & Assign Workflows:**\
"Create a new run of the Quarterly Access Review workflow and assign it to the compliance manager [\[email protected\]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)"

**Summarize & Interact with Workflow Runs:**\
"Find the most recent Client Onboarding workflow runs and show me where there are bottlenecks from incomplete tasks"

**Search & Modify Data Sets:**\
"Search Data Set records for active vendors and, if they haven't been reviewed in the past 6 months, mark them as Review"

**Manage Tasks:**\
"Show me all overdue tasks across my workflows and assign them to the appropriate team members"
