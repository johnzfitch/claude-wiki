Enhance responses with scholarly research and citations. The Scholar Gateway Connector grounds Claude's responses in peer-reviewed sources with verifiable citations and DOI links, helping you ensure your Claude-assisted research meets professional research standards.\

You can use the Scholar Gateway connector to:

Support literature reviews and academic writing:\
"I'm researching marine ecosystem responses to microplastic pollution. Find recent studies from 2021–2025 on detection methods, uptake mechanisms, and ecosystem impacts. What does current research tell us about these areas? Provide citations so I can dig deeper into the sources"

Strengthen grant proposals with recent research:\
"I'm writing a grant proposal on developing next-generation battery technologies for energy storage. What does recent research (2022–2025) tell us about solid-state batteries, particularly their safety advantages and manufacturing challenges?"

Find cross-disciplinary research connections:\
"I'm a researcher working on clean energy policy effectiveness. What does recent research tell us about why people choose (or don't choose) to adopt renewable energy like solar panels? Please include studies from behavioral economics, psychology, and policy research from 2020–2025"
