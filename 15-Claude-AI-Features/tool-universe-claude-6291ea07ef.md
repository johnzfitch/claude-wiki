The ToolUniverse connector provides an ecosystem for creating AI scientist systems, integrating more than 700 machine learning models, datasets, APIs, and scientific packages for data analysis, knowledge retrieval, and experimental design. It standardizes how Claude identifies and calls scientific tools across bioinformatics, cheminformatics, genomics, structural biology, proteomics, and drug discovery.

ToolUniverse transforms Claude into a powerful research scientist by abstracting scientific capabilities behind a unified interface—enabling tool discovery, execution, and composition into multi-step workflows without additional configuration.

You can use the ToolUniverse connector to:

**Discover Scientific Tools:** "Find protein structure prediction tools"

**Query Biological Databases:** "Get the function of UniProt protein P05067"

**Research Drug Targets:** "Find disease target associations for type 2 diabetes"

**Execute Computational Biology Workflows:** "Search for EGFR inhibitors and retrieve their bioactivity data from ChEMBL"

**Compose Multi-Step Research Pipelines:** "Identify drug targets for Alzheimer's disease, then find approved drugs that interact with those targets"\
\
Note: ToolUniverse is only available on Claude desktop app.
