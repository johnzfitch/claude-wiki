In this video guide, we'll walk you through an example of using Claude's Research feature to plan a team offsite.

Research is available for users with paid Claude plans (Pro, Max, Team, or Enterprise) using Claude on the web, Claude Desktop, or Claude Mobile.

You must have web search turned on for Research to function.
