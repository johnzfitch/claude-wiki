Turn your Claude agent into a prospecting platform. Find any company or professional right in your Claude. Get contact information, roles, tech stack, business events, website changes, Intent data, and much more. Build lead lists, research prospects, identify talent, and craft personalized outreach, all without leaving your chat. Vibe Prospecting delivers fresh B2B data backed by 50+ data sources. Vibe Prospecting has 150M+ companies and 800M+ professionals, with verified contact information, firmographics, technographics, hiring trends, intent signals, funding events, and more.

You can use Vibe Prospecting to:

**Build Lead Lists:\**
"Find 50 SaaS companies in California with 100-500 employees that raised Series B funding in the last 6 months"

**Research Prospects:\**
"Get contact details and recent company news for the VP of Sales at Acme Corp"

**Identify Talent:\**
"Find senior software engineers at fintech companies in New York who previously worked at Goldman Sachs"
