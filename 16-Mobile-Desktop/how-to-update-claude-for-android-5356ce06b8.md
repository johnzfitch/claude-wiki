If you aren’t seeing the latest features on Claude for Android, you may need to update your mobile app manually by following these instructions from Google:

Open the Google Play Store app .

At the top right, tap the profile icon.

Tap “Manage apps & device.”

Under Updates available, tap “See details.”

Tap “Update” next to Claude for Android.
