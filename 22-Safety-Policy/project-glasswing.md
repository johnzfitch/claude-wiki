# Project Glasswing: Securing Critical Software for the AI Era

Project Glasswing is Anthropic's cross-industry initiative to secure the world's most critical software, powered by Claude Mythos Preview — Anthropic's most capable AI model for cybersecurity tasks.

Announced on April 7, 2026.

## Overview

Project Glasswing partners the organizations responsible for infrastructure that billions of people depend on with Claude Mythos Preview, giving their defenders a head start in the coming AI-driven era of cybersecurity.

Anthropic is not making Claude Mythos Preview generally available due to its cybersecurity capabilities. Instead, access is restricted to vetted partners who build or maintain critical software infrastructure.

- **$4M** in direct donations to open-source security organizations:
  - $2.5M to Alpha-Omega and the Open Source Security Foundation through the Linux Foundation
  - $1.5M to the Apache Software Foundation

## Claude Mythos Preview

Claude Mythos Preview is a new general-purpose language model that sits in a fourth model tier named **Copybara**. Anthropic describes it as superior to any other existing AI frontier model.

### Capabilities

- Capable of identifying and exploiting zero-day vulnerabilities in every major operating system and every major web browser
- Has autonomously discovered thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities during testing
- Can chain together 3, 4, or sometimes 5 vulnerabilities in sequence to produce sophisticated exploits
- Demonstrates "a level of coding capability where [it] can surpass all but the most skilled humans at finding and exploiting software vulnerabilities"

### Benchmarks

| Benchmark | Score |
|-----------|-------|
| SWE-bench Verified | 93.9% |
| GPQA Diamond | 94.6% |
| CyberGym | 83.1% |

Highest-scoring model on record across all three benchmarks.

### Availability

- **Not publicly available** — restricted to Project Glasswing partners and vetted organizations
- Available through the Claude API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry for participants
- Pricing described as "very expensive" but specific per-token pricing not publicly disclosed

## Why Limited Access

As Newton Cheng, Frontier Red Team Cyber Lead at Anthropic, explained: Anthropic is not making the model generally available due to concerns that it may pose unprecedented cybersecurity risks and could increase the likelihood of large-scale AI-driven cyberattacks. The restricted access is designed to give defenders a head start before the capabilities become more widely available.
