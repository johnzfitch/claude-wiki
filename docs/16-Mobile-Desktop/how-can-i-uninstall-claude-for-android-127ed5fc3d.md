To uninstall the Claude app from your Android device, hold down on the app from your screen until you see the menu options. Click on “App Info.” From the next series of options, select “Uninstall.” 

 

Note that if you have an active Claude Pro subscription and would like to uninstall the app, your subscription will not be automatically canceled. To cancel your subscription, follow the cancellation instructions here.
