As a Primary Owner or Owner of a Claude for Work (Team or Enterprise) plan, you can manage the ability for members of your organization to submit feedback to Anthropic via thumbs up / thumbs down.

Navigate to Admin settings > Data and Privacy

Click “Enable” or “Disable” to change the Thumbs feedback setting for your organization:

 

More information on how Anthropic collects, uses, and stores feedback data can be found in our Privacy Center: How long do you store my organization’s data?
