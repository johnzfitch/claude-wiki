# Claude for Product Management

Updated this week

In this video guide, we'll explore several examples of how Claude can be used by product management teams. Before you get started, review the availability of the features demonstrated in this video:

- Artifacts are available on all Claude.ai plans.

- Projects are available on Claude Pro and Claude for Work (Team & Enterprise plans).

- Project sharing features are available on Claude for Work (Team & Enterprise plans). More information on project visibility and sharing can be found [here](https://support.anthropic.com/en/articles/9519189-project-visibility-and-sharing).
